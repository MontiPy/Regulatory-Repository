### Practical recommendation

Start with **U.S., EU, Australia, Japan, Korea, Canada, and New Zealand** because they have the clearest API/XML paths. Then build monitored-source connectors for **UNECE, China, GCC/GSO, Brazil, Mexico, India, and EAEU**. This gives you a working automated backbone quickly, while still keeping broad-scope markets visible for manual review.
