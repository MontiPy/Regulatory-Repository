# Road vehicles - Functional safety

**Regulated Area:** Functional safety / E-E safety case

**Applicability:** Not generally a direct government regulation, but widely expected as evidence for E/E safety in ADAS, braking, steering, EV and software-intensive passenger vehicles.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide a structured process to identify and control E/E malfunction hazards.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

E/E architecture, ECUs, telematics, gateways, ADAS/automated-driving controllers, diagnostics, software release records, update backend and cybersecurity controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Uncontrolled E/E fault, latent safety fault, degraded fail-safe behavior, incomplete safety case, approval or audit challenge.

## Related Regulations

EU GSR2; UN R157; brake/steering/ADAS approvals; ISO 21448.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence; not a substitute for each government regulation.
