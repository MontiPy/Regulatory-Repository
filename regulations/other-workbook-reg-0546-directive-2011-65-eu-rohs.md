# Materials / Electronics - RoHS

**Regulated Area:** Materials / recycling / restricted substances

**Applicability:** Applies to electrical and electronic equipment within scope; passenger vehicle relevance is conditional and often overlaps with ELV exemptions, accessories and service equipment.


## Key Compliance Intent

Control restricted substances in electrical/electronic equipment and clarify overlap with vehicle ELV obligations.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Electrical/electronic modules, accessories, chargers/service equipment where in scope, solder, connectors, PCBs, displays, sensors and supplier material declarations.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Restricted substance exceedance, wrong exemption assumption, missing supplier declaration, blocked accessory or service equipment sale, audit finding.

## Related Regulations

EU ELV; REACH; WEEE; POPs; China RoHS.
