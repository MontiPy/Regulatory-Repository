# Canada Radiofrequency and EMC Equipment Approval

**Regulated Area:** Telecom / RF / EMC

**Applicability:** Applies to radio apparatus and interference-causing equipment installed in vehicles; passenger relevance depends on fitted RF/EMC devices.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure radio devices fitted to passenger vehicles comply with spectrum, EMC and equipment approval rules in each market.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Telematics, keyless entry, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi/cellular modules, radar, tire-pressure sensors, antennas, infotainment, RF harnesses and labels.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Illegal transmitter configuration, RF interference, non-approved module, labeling/documentation gap, degraded keyless/telematics/radar function, market release hold.

## Related Regulations

FCC Part 15; EU RED; China SRRC; Japan MIC/TELEC; Korea RRA; Brazil ANATEL

## Engineering Considerations

Add to RF compliance gate for telematics, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth, keyless entry, radar and TPMS.
