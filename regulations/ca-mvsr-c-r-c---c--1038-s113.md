113(1)In this section, hood means any exterior movable body panel forward of the windshield that is used to cover an engine, luggage, storage or battery compartment.(2)Each hood with which a vehicle is equipped shall be provided with a hood latch system.(3)A front-opening hood that, in any position, partially or completely obstructs a driver’s forward view through the windshield of the vehicle shall be provided with a second latching position on the hood latch system or with a second hood latch system.SOR/79-306, s. 4; SOR/79-940, s. 7; SOR/2000-182, s. 6
