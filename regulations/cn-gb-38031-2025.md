# Electric Vehicles Traction Battery Safety Requirements

**Regulated Area:** EV battery and high-voltage safety

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Protect occupants, service personnel, and first responders from shock, fire, and chemical hazards from EV propulsion systems.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Traction battery pack, cells/modules, BMS, contactors, HV harness, service disconnect, onboard charger, DC/DC, inverter, thermal management.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Battery thermal event, loss of high-voltage isolation, exposed live parts, electrolyte leakage, contactor weld, charging fault, post-crash reignition.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 305/305a; UNECE R100; GB 18384/38031; KMVSS Arts. 18-2/18-3/91-3/112-14; ADR 109; CONTRAN 749; GSO MC-250905; Japan Article 17-2.

## Engineering Considerations

Updated traction battery safety standard; effective date should be built into battery program gates.
