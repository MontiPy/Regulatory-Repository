## Article 9 — Minimum Turning Radius

① The minimum turning radius of a motor vehicle, measured along the centerline of the outer front wheel track, shall not exceed 12 meters. [Amended 2012-02-15]

② Notwithstanding paragraph ①, for buses, the vehicle body shall not simultaneously contact both concentric circles of radius 5.3 meters and 12.5 meters when turning. [Added 2012-02-15, 2020-02-28]

[Fully amended 1999-06-28]
