# Motor Vehicles - Methods of Test for Impact Strength - Part 4: Roof Strength

**Regulated Area:** Roof strength / ejection mitigation

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce injury in rollover and partial ejection events by strengthening the roof and retaining occupants.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Roof rails, pillars, windshield/header, door frames, side curtain airbags, laminated side glass, seat belts.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Excessive roof intrusion, side curtain does not cover openings, occupant ejection, glazing release, belt retention shortfall.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 216/226; GSO 39; KMVSS Art. 92; side curtain/ejection requirements may overlap side impact rules.

## Engineering Considerations

Listed in GSO MY2027 technical regulations; passenger rollover relevance.
