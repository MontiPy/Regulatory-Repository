## Article 39 — Reversing Lamps

Motor vehicles (excluding towed vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 0.75 tonnes or less) shall be equipped with reversing lamps meeting the following standards: [Amended 2018-07-11, 2022-10-26]

1. The following number shall be installed at the rear of the vehicle. However, in the case of subitem (b), two additional lamps may be installed at the rear, or one additional lamp at each rear side:

a. Vehicles up to 6 metres in length: 1 or 2 lamps

b. Vehicles exceeding 6 metres in length: 2 lamps

2. The light colour shall be white.

3. The installation and luminous intensity standards for reversing lamps shall meet Attached Table 6-10. However, ultra-compact vehicles may apply the standards in Attached Table 40.

[Fully amended 2014-06-10]
