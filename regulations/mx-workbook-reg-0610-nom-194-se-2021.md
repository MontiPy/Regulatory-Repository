# Safety devices for new light vehicles - requirements and specifications

**Regulated Area:** Market access / safety devices

**Applicability:** Applies to new light vehicles sold in Mexico; passenger-vehicle relevance for required safety devices and evidence recognition.


## Key Compliance Intent

Set minimum safety-device requirements for light passenger vehicles sold in Mexico and define acceptable compliance evidence.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Restraints, ESC, brakes, lighting, glazing, child restraints, tires, labels, owner information and homologation evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Missing required safety device, lack of accepted UNECE/FMVSS evidence, label/manual gap, blocked import/sale, delayed launch.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS; UNECE regulations; NOM-042; NOM-163.

## Engineering Considerations

Corrects Claude note that referenced NOM-237; NOM-237-SE-2021 is not vehicle safety. NOM-236-SE-2021 was a vehicle inspection standard but has been cancelled.
