F2006L0229921 July 2006

This standard was originally made under section 7 of the [Motor Vehicle Standards Act 1989](/C2004A03813) and from 1 July 2021 continues in force as if it were a national road vehicle standard determined under section 12 of the [Road Vehicle Standards Act 2018](/C2018A00163) (see Schedule 3, Part 2, item 2 of the [Road Vehicle Standards (Consequential and Transitional Provisions) Act 2018](/C2018A00164)).

This Standard repeals each vehicle standard with the name Australian Design Rule 63/00 — Trailers Designed for Use in Road Trains that is: (a) made under section 7 of the Motor Vehicle Standards Act 1989; and (b) in force at the commencement of this Standard. This Standard also repeals each instrument made under section 7 of the Motor Vehicle Standards Act 1989 that creates a vehicle standard with the name Australian Design Rule
