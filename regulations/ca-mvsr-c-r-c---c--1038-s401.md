401(1)Every passenger car and three-wheeled vehicle shall conform to the requirements for passenger cars set out in Technical Standards Document No. 401, Interior Trunk Release (TSD 401), as amended from time to time.(2)However, vehicles manufactured before September 1, 2010, need not be equipped with an interior trunk release.(3)This section does not apply to vehicles equipped with a back door.
