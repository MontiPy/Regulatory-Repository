# Electrically propelled road vehicles - Safety specifications

**Regulated Area:** EV battery and high-voltage safety evidence

**Applicability:** Not generally a direct regulation, but supports design and validation evidence for EV high-voltage safety in passenger vehicles.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide design guidance and evidence for safe electric powertrain systems.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

HV battery, HV wiring, contactors, inverter, motor, charging inlet, isolation monitoring, service disconnects, labels and barriers.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Loss of HV isolation, exposed live parts, missing warning label, failed HVIL, unsafe service condition, approval evidence gap.

## Related Regulations

UN R100; FMVSS 305; GB 38031; AIS-038; ADR 109.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence for EV safety rows.
