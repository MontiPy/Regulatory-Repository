# Automatic emergency call equipment requirements linked to EAEU/Russia vehicle approval

**Regulated Area:** Emergency call / telematics / market access

**Applicability:** Potentially applies to passenger vehicles for Russia/EAEU market entry depending on category and effective date; verify current enforcement and exemptions.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure vehicle occupants can trigger or automatically initiate emergency response after a crash where the market requires ERA-GLONASS.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Telematics control unit, GNSS receiver, cellular modem, crash-signal input, backup power, microphone/speaker, eCall button, backend service interface.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Emergency call not initiated, wrong location transmitted, TCU registration failure, backup power failure, market approval blocked.

## Related Regulations

EU eCall; UN R144; Gulf emergency call where adopted.

## Engineering Considerations

Claude flagged ERA-GLONASS as unique for EAEU/Russia; maintain as market-signoff item.
