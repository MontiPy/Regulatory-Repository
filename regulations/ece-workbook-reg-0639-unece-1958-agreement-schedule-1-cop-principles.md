# UNECE type approval and conformity-of-production framework

**Regulated Area:** Type approval / CoP / market surveillance

**Applicability:** Applies where a Contracting Party uses UNECE approvals; passenger vehicle relevance for type approval, CoP controls and approval marking across many UN Regulations.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger vehicles and regulated systems remain consistent with approved type and accepted UN Regulation evidence.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Approval certificates, extension files, CoP control plans, production inspection records, component markings and technical-service evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Approval extension missed, production variant outside approved specification, CoP finding, component marking mismatch, recall or market-surveillance action.

## Related Regulations

EU 2018/858; national type approval frameworks; UN Regulations by subject area.

## Engineering Considerations

Added because the guide lists many UN Regulations but should also capture the framework that controls approval evidence and CoP.
