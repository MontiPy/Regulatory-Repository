# The Protection of the Occupants in the Event of a Lateral Collision

**Regulated Area:** Side impact

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Protect occupants where crush space is limited and prevent intrusion-related injury in side crashes.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Doors, sills, B-pillar, seats, side airbags, curtain airbags, side sensors, latch/hinge hardware, battery/fuel packaging.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

High thorax/pelvis/head injury values, excessive door intrusion, side airbag non-deployment, curtain coverage gap, door opening in crash.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 214; UNECE R95/R135; GB 20071; KMVSS Arts. 102-4/104; ADR 72/85; CONTRAN 949; GSO 38/1707/1708; Japan Article 18.

## Engineering Considerations

New mandatory side collision standard published with future implementation; confirm transition from GB 20071-2006 by launch date.
