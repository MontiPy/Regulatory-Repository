# Requirements for Prohibited Substances on Automobiles

**Regulated Area:** Materials / restricted substances

**Applicability:** Applies to automobiles and auto parts used in China as a prohibited-substance reference; verify whether compulsory or customer/approval-triggered for the specific program.


## Key Compliance Intent

Control hazardous substances and support recycling/end-of-life obligations for vehicles and components.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Materials, coatings, electronics, batteries, plastics, rubber, interior trim, supplier material declarations, recycling/disassembly information.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Restricted substance exceedance, missing IMDS/material data, blocked vehicle approval or sale, recycling fee/reporting error, part redesign late in launch.

## Related Regulations

EU REACH; EU ELV; EU RoHS; China RoHS GB 26572

## Engineering Considerations

Recommended GB/T status, but often important for China market material compliance and supplier declarations.
