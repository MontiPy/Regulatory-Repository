## Article 102-4 — Pole Side Impact Safety

Passenger cars and trucks with a gross vehicle weight of 3.5 tonnes or less shall meet the pole side impact standards in Attached Table 14-8. However, the standards in the main provision do not apply to the following vehicles. [Amended 2022-10-26]

1. Low-speed electric vehicles

2. Ultra-compact vehicles

3. Forward-cab trucks with a gross vehicle weight of 3.5 tonnes or less

[Added 2018-12-31]
