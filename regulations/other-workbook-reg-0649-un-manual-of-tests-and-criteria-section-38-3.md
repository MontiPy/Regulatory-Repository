# Lithium battery transport tests

**Regulated Area:** Battery logistics / transport safety

**Applicability:** Mandatory for transport of lithium cells/batteries in many logistics regimes; passenger vehicle relevance for HV batteries, modules, service parts and prototypes shipped separately or in vehicles.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure lithium batteries can be safely transported and accepted by carriers/regulators.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Cells, modules, battery packs, service batteries, prototype batteries, packaging, shipping documents and logistics processes.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Shipping hold, battery vent/fire during logistics, missing UN38.3 summary, damaged prototype shipment, customs/carrier rejection.

## Related Regulations

EU Battery Regulation; ADR/RID/IMDG/IATA dangerous goods rules; GB 38031.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting/logistics requirement because it affects EV program timing and service parts.
