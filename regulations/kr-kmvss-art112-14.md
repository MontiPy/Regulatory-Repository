## Article 112-14 — Traction Battery for Electric Vehicles

Traction batteries used in electric vehicles and two-wheeled vehicles powered by electric energy (excluding two-wheeled vehicles powered by electric energy generated from hydrogen) shall meet the standards in each item of Article 18-3.
