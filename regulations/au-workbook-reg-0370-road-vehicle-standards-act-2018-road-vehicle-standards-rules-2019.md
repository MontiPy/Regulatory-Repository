# Road Vehicle Standards - Supply, Type Approval and Compliance Framework

**Regulated Area:** Market entry / type approval / certification

**Applicability:** Applies to road vehicles supplied to the Australian market, including new and imported passenger vehicles, through approvals, entry on the Register of Approved Vehicles and compliance with applicable ADRs.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger vehicles supplied in Australia meet national road vehicle safety, anti-theft and emissions standards.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Vehicle type approval, ADR applicability matrix, RAV data, VIN, compliance evidence, import approvals, conformity of production controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Vehicle cannot be supplied; RAV entry missing or wrong; ADR evidence incomplete; VIN/configuration mismatch; imported vehicle held or recalled.

## Related Regulations

ADR technical standards; EU type approval; 49 CFR import/certification rules; CMVSS certification.

## Engineering Considerations

Original guide captured individual ADRs but not the overarching Australian supply/compliance framework.
