# Limits and Measurement Methods for Emissions from Light-duty Vehicles (China 6)

**Regulated Area:** Emissions

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Limit pollutants from passenger vehicles over useful life and ensure emission control faults are detected.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Engine, catalysts, particulate filters, EGR, SCR/urea, evaporative system, OBD, fuel system, calibration software.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Excess NOx/HC/CO/PM, MIL/OBD noncompliance, evaporative leaks, catalyst deterioration, defeat-risk calibration behavior.

## Related Regulations

US EPA 40 CFR Part 86/Tier 3; EU Reg. 715/2007 and UNECE R83; GB 18352.6; ADR 79/111/112; CONAMA 492/IBAMA IN 3; GSO 1680-series and national Euro limits; Japan Article 31; KMVSS Art. 36.

## Engineering Considerations

China 6 light-duty passenger vehicle emissions; verify China 6a/6b and local implementation timing.
