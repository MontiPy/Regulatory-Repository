# Symbols for Controls, Indicators and Tell-tales of Motor Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Controls / HMI

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure the driver can identify and operate controls and understand vehicle warnings without ambiguity.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Controls, tell-tales, indicators, displays, HMI software, instrument cluster, switches, warning lamps.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Wrong or missing tell-tales, unreadable symbols, incorrect lamp color, delayed warnings, driver confusion during safety-critical events.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 101; GB 4094; KMVSS Art. 13; ADR 18; SASO 3004; CONTRAN 758; Japan Article 10.

## Engineering Considerations

Controls/tell-tales/symbols standard for road vehicles.
