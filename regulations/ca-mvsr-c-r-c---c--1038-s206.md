206(1)Every bus with a GVWR of 4 536 kg or less, enclosed motorcycle, multi-purpose passenger vehicle, three-wheeled vehicle, passenger car and truck that is equipped with side doors or back doors shall be so equipped in accordance with either(a)the requirements of Technical Standards Document No. 206, Door Locks and Door Retention Components (TSD 206), as amended from time to time; or(b)the general requirements, performance requirements and test procedures set out in United Nations Regulation No. 11, Uniform Provisions Concerning the Approval of Vehicles with regard to Door Latches and Door Retention Components (United Nations Regulation No. 11), as amended from time to time by any amendment in the 03 or 04 series of amendments.(2)For the purposes of paragraph 6.3.2 of United Nations Regulation No. 11, the locking device shall be the device referred to in paragraph 6.3.2.1(a) or (b) of that Regulation.(3)[Repealed, SOR/2014-307, s. 16]SOR/97-14, s. 2; SOR/2001-116, s. 3; SOR/2002-55, s. 21; SOR/2003-272, s. 23; SOR/2005-42, ss. 7, 8; SOR/2006-94, s. 4(E); SOR/2008-73, s. 3; SOR/2009-34, s. 1; SOR/2013-220, s. 2; SOR/2014-307, s. 16; SOR/2017-57, ss. 6, 8
