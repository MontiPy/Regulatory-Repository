# India emission and fuel-consumption compliance framework

**Regulated Area:** Emissions / fuel economy / fleet compliance

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles sold in India where emissions and fuel consumption standards apply; exact limits depend on fuel type, model year and vehicle category.


## Key Compliance Intent

Limit environmental impact and ensure passenger vehicles and fleets meet certified emissions, fuel economy and reporting obligations.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Engine, aftertreatment, evaporative system, OBD, calibration, fuel/energy consumption data, hybrid/EV powertrain data, road-load data, fleet compliance ledger.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Excess NOx/CO/HC/PM/PN or CO2, evaporative leak, OBD monitor gap, inaccurate road-load data, fleet credit shortfall, late report, certificate or market-access hold.

## Related Regulations

EPA Tier 3; Euro 6/7; China 6; Japan fuel economy; Brazil PROCONVE; Mexico NOM-042/NOM-163.

## Engineering Considerations

Framework row for India environmental compliance; split into detailed Bharat Stage and fuel-consumption standards during market sign-off.
