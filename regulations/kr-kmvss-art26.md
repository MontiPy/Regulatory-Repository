## Article 26 — Head Restraints

The front seats (excluding middle seats) of the following vehicles shall be equipped with head restraints capable of reducing impact to the occupant's head in the event of a rear collision: [Amended 2018-07-11, 2024-11-29]

1. Passenger cars (excluding ultra-compact passenger cars)

2. Vans with a gross vehicle weight of 4.5 tonnes or less

3. Trucks with a gross vehicle weight of 4.5 tonnes or less (excluding ultra-compact trucks and towed vehicles)

4. Special-purpose vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 4.5 tonnes or less (excluding ultra-compact special-purpose vehicles)

[Fully amended 2010-03-29]
