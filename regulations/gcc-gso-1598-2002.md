# Head Restraints for Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Seats and head restraints

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure seats remain attached and support the occupant and head/neck during normal use and crashes.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Seat structures, tracks, recliners, head restraints, seatback locks, adjusters, integrated restraints, second-row mechanisms.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Seat track release, seatback collapse, head restraint too low or too far back, excessive rearward rotation, occupant ramping.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 202/207; UNECE R17/R25; GB 15083; KMVSS Arts. 25/26/97/99; ADR 3/22; CONTRAN 951; GSO 1598; Japan Articles 22/22-4.

Passenger seat/head restraint relevance; verify exact vehicle categories and test references.
