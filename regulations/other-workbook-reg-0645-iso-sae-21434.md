# Road vehicles - Cybersecurity engineering

**Regulated Area:** Cybersecurity / CSMS evidence

**Applicability:** Not a regulation by itself, but commonly used as the engineering process basis behind UN R155 and other cyber approval regimes.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide engineering evidence that cybersecurity risks are identified and managed across the vehicle lifecycle.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

E/E architecture, ECUs, telematics, gateways, ADAS/automated-driving controllers, diagnostics, software release records, update backend and cybersecurity controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Unauthorized access, missing TARA, untracked vulnerability, supplier cyber evidence gap, CSMS approval challenge.

## Related Regulations

UN R155; GB 44495; ISO 24089; EU GSR2.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence; link to cyber requirements in market approval matrix.
