## Article 18-3 — Traction Battery

The traction battery of a motor vehicle shall comply with the following standards: [Amended 2013-03-23]

1. It shall be separated from the passenger compartment by a wall or protective panel.

2. It shall be equipped with a function to prevent overcharging beyond the designed range and to cut off excessive current.

3. It shall not ignite or explode under the physical, chemical, electrical, and thermal shock conditions published by the Minister of Land, Infrastructure and Transport.

[Added 2009-01-23]
