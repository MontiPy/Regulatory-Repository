## Article 111-2 — Operational Design Domain for Autonomous Driving Systems

① The manufacturer shall designate an operational domain in which the autonomous driving system can operate normally and safely under given conditions (hereafter “Operational Design Domain”).

② The Operational Design Domain shall include the following matters relating to the operation of the autonomous vehicle:

1. Operating environment including road and weather conditions.

2. Operational limits of the autonomous driving system.

3. Other conditions related to the safe operation of the vehicle.
