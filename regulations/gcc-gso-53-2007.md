# Passenger Car Tyres - Part 3: Methods of Test

**Regulated Area:** Tires and wheels

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure tires/wheels can carry vehicle loads safely and that drivers are warned about unsafe pressure.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Passenger car tires, wheels/rims, valve stems, TPMS, spare tire, placards, load/speed rating selection.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Tread separation, blowout, bead unseating, rim mismatch, missing/wrong placard, TPMS no-warning or false warning.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 110; UNECE R30/R64/R141; GB 9743; KMVSS Arts. 12/12-2/112-11; ADR 23/95; CONTRAN 913; GSO 51/52/53; Japan Article 9.

## Engineering Considerations

Passenger car tyre test methods.
