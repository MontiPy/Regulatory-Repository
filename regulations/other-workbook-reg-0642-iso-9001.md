# Quality management systems - Requirements

**Regulated Area:** Quality management / governance

**Applicability:** Not a vehicle regulation by itself; may support governance where IATF is not mandated or for corporate/supplier quality systems.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide a baseline QMS for controlled product realization and compliance evidence.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Quality system, document control, process audits, supplier controls, corrective action and management review.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Incomplete quality records, uncontrolled procedures, late corrective actions, supplier escape, weak CoP evidence.

## Related Regulations

IATF 16949; CoP frameworks; customer-specific requirements.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence, not a legal vehicle standard.
