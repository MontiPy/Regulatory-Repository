# Register of Approved Vehicles Entry Requirements

**Regulated Area:** Certification / traceability / market entry

**Applicability:** Applies before road vehicles may be provided to the Australian market; passenger vehicles require correct RAV entry when supplied under applicable approval pathways.


## Key Compliance Intent

Create a national database showing vehicles approved for the Australian market.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

VIN, vehicle variant/version data, approval number, compliance data feed, plant release process, import/sales systems.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

RAV entry missing; VIN not recognized; wrong variant/approval data; vehicle cannot be registered or supplied; stop-sale at first supply.

## Related Regulations

Road Vehicle Standards Act/Rules; ADRs; VIN/certification frameworks in other regions.

## Engineering Considerations

Operationally important for Australian launch, registration and first-supply controls.
