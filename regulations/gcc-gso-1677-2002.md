# Motor Vehicles - Laminated Safety Glass

**Regulated Area:** Glazing and visibility

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Maintain transparent, durable, and safe glazing while preserving driver visibility and crash retention.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Windshield, side/rear glazing, laminated glass, adhesives, tint, camera/sensor optical zones, sunroof glazing.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Reduced driver visibility, glazing shatter pattern nonconformance, low light transmittance, windshield separation, camera misread due optical distortion.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 205/212/219; UNECE R43; GB 9656; KMVSS Arts. 34/105/112-7; ADR 8; CONTRAN 960; GSO 1677; Japan Article 29.

## Engineering Considerations

Passenger windshield/laminated glazing relevance.
