## Article 112-2 — Brake Hoses

Brake hoses used in motor vehicles (excluding ultra-compact vehicles) shall meet the standards in Attached Table 30-4, and brake hoses other than air pressure brake hoses shall be of an integral non-separable construction. [Amended 2018-07-11]
