# Performance Requirements and Test Methods for Windshield Wiping and Washing Systems

**Regulated Area:** Wipers and washers

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Allow the driver to clear the windshield reliably in rain, dirt, snow, and spray.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Wiper motor/linkage/arms/blades, washer pump, nozzles, reservoir, controls, rain sensor, heated nozzles.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Reduced driver visibility, insufficient wiped area, streaking, wiper stall, nozzle clog/freeze, washer flow failure.

FMVSS/CMVSS 104; GB 15085; KMVSS Art. 109; ADR 42; CONTRAN 224; SASO 3007; Japan Article 45.
