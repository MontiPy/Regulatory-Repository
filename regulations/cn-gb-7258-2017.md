# Technical Specifications for Safety of Power-driven Vehicles Operating on Roads

**Regulated Area:** General safety / conformity

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure the vehicle placed on the market matches the approved or self-certified specification and can be traced.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Whole vehicle, certification labels, VIN, conformity documentation, production quality controls, recall and field action systems.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Incorrect certification label, wrong configuration built, missing VIN, documentation mismatch, delayed recall, market access failure.

## Related Regulations

GSO 42/48; FMVSS/CMVSS labels and VIN standards; ADR type approval; Japan type designation; Brazil CONTRAN equipment and registration rules.

## Engineering Considerations

Broad road-vehicle safety specification; use as baseline for China market compliance mapping.
