## Article 103-2 — Child Restraint Anchorage System

Child restraint anchorage systems installed in passenger cars (excluding ultra-compact passenger cars) shall meet the following standards. [Amended 2018-07-11]

1. Shall meet the standards in Article 27-2.

2. The upper anchorage shall be constructed to withstand a longitudinal load of 816 kilograms for 0.2 seconds or more, and displacement shall be within 125 millimetres.

3. The lower anchorage shall be constructed to withstand a longitudinal load of 816 kilograms and a load of 510 kilograms applied at 75 degrees to the longitudinal direction, each for 0.2 seconds or more, and displacement shall be within 125 millimetres.

[Added 2008-01-14]
