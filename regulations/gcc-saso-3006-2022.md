# Road Vehicles - Accelerator Control Systems

**Regulated Area:** Powertrain / accelerator control

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent unintended propulsion and ensure predictable driver command response.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Accelerator pedal, throttle body, powertrain controller, return springs, cruise/regen logic, transmission interlocks.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Pedal does not return, throttle remains open, unintended acceleration, inconsistent regenerative braking request, MIL or limp-home activation.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 102, 124; SASO 3006; Japan Article 10; KMVSS controls/powertrain articles.

## Engineering Considerations

Saudi national requirement listed in GSO annex; applies when exporting to Saudi Arabia.
