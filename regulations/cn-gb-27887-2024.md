# Child Restraint Systems for Motor Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Child restraint compatibility

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Allow child restraints to be installed correctly and remain attached under crash loading.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

ISOFIX/LATCH lower anchors, top tether anchors, child restraint fit zones, seat belt geometry, labels, built-in child restraints.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Inaccessible anchors, anchor pull-out, top tether failure, poor CRS fit, incorrect label, excessive child dummy head/chest loads.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS 213/225; CMVSS 210.1/210.2/213.4; UNECE R129/R145; GB 27887; KMVSS Arts. 27-2/103-2; ADR 34; GSO 1709 and ECE 44/129/145 annex; Japan Article 22-5.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory CRS standard; vehicle teams must coordinate child seat compatibility and owner information.
