# Flammability of Automotive Interior Materials

**Regulated Area:** Materials / recycling / restricted substances

**Applicability:** Interior trim, textiles, foam, insulation, adhesives, coatings, carpets, service materials Retained under broad-scope review so the program team can manually disposition applicability.
