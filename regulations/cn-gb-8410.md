# Flammability of Automotive Interior Materials

**Regulated Area:** Materials / recycling / restricted substances

**Applicability:** Interior trim, textiles, foam, insulation, adhesives, coatings, carpets, service materials Retained under broad-scope review so the program team can manually disposition applicability.


## Key Compliance Intent

Control hazardous substances and support recycling/end-of-life obligations for vehicles and components.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Materials, coatings, electronics, batteries, plastics, rubber, interior trim, supplier material declarations, recycling/disassembly information.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Restricted substance exceedance, missing IMDS/material data, blocked vehicle approval or sale, recycling fee/reporting error, part redesign late in launch.

## Related Regulations

Possible functional overlap: GB / China GB 9656-2021 - Safety Technical Specification for Glazing Materials Used in Power-driven Vehicles

## Engineering Considerations

Regional passenger/light-vehicle coverage; confirm exact applicability by vehicle category, model year, fuel/propulsion type, and local type-approval route.
