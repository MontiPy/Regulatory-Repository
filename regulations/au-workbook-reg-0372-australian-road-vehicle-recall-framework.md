# Vehicle Recalls Under Road Vehicle Standards and Consumer Product Safety Framework

**Regulated Area:** Recall / field action / market surveillance

**Applicability:** Applies to road vehicles and components supplied in Australia, including passenger vehicles, where safety defects or noncompliances require recall action.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure safety-related vehicle defects and noncompliances in the Australian market are corrected and consumers are informed.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Field quality data, recall population, VIN list, dealer remedy instructions, owner notifications, regulator reporting.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Safety defect not escalated; owner notification incomplete; wrong VIN population; remedy parts not available; regulator action or compulsory recall.

## Related Regulations

49 CFR Parts 573/577; EU market surveillance; Canada defect/non-compliance notices; Brazil SENATRAN/CONTRAN recall processes.

## Engineering Considerations

Keep as a horizontal field-quality item separate from individual ADR performance requirements.
