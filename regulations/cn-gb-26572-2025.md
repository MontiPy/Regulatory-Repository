# Requirements for Restricted Use of Hazardous Substances in Electrical and Electronic Products

**Regulated Area:** Materials / electronics / restricted substances

**Applicability:** Horizontal electronics/RoHS-style requirement; passenger-vehicle relevance depends on in-vehicle electronic products and implementation scope; verify official applicability and transition dates.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent fire, explosion, shock and hazardous leakage during normal use, abuse and post-crash conditions.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Fuel system, tanks, lines, evaporative components, HV battery/REESS, contactors, service disconnect, inverters, hydrogen/CNG storage and crash shutoff controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fuel leakage after impact, battery thermal event, loss of high-voltage isolation, electrolyte leakage, contactor weld, pressure relief malfunction, fire risk.

## Related Regulations

EU RoHS; GB/T 30512; supplier material declarations

## Engineering Considerations

Added as broad-scope watch item due electronics content in passenger vehicles; verify automotive exclusions or coverage before treating as mandatory for vehicle-level approval.
