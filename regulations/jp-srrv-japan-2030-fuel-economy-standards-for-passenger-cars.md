# Japan 2030 Fuel Economy Standards for Passenger Cars

**Regulated Area:** Fuel economy / CO2 / electrification

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger cars, including gasoline, diesel, LPG, battery electric and plug-in hybrid vehicles under the stated Japanese 2030 fuel economy framework.


## Key Compliance Intent

Improve passenger-car energy efficiency and reduce CO2 through Japan's 2030 target framework.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Powertrain, hybrid/EV system, battery, aerodynamics, mass, tires, road-load data, energy consumption and range data.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fuel economy target shortfall; EV/PHEV energy data mismatch; road-load error; fleet mix noncompliance; late powertrain calibration change reduces compliance margin.

## Related Regulations

WLTC/WLTP frameworks; EU CO2 rules; U.S. CAFE/GHG; Canada GHG; China CAFC/NEV requirements.
