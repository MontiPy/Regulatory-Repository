# Road Vehicle - Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)

**Regulated Area:** Certification / traceability / recall readiness

**Applicability:** Applies to road vehicles in China, including passenger vehicles, for VIN content and identification requirements.


## Key Compliance Intent

Standardize VIN identification for registration, traceability, recalls and enforcement.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

VIN marking, vehicle identification plate, body/frame marking, ECU/build records, recall population database.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Duplicate VIN; invalid VIN structure; marking unreadable; VIN mismatch between vehicle and documents; recall or registration population error.

## Related Regulations

49 CFR Part 565; ISO 3779/3780; CMVSS/MVSR VIN; ADR/RAV VIN; Japan/Korea VIN requirements.

## Engineering Considerations

Added because passenger-vehicle traceability standards belong in the revised scope.
