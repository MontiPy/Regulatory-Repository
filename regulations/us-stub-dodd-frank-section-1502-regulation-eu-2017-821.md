# Conflict minerals and responsible sourcing due-diligence frameworks

**Regulated Area:** Materials / supply-chain due diligence

**Applicability:** Applies to companies in scope of U.S./EU conflict minerals rules; passenger-vehicle relevance for electronics, batteries and supplier due diligence.


## Key Compliance Intent

Support responsible sourcing and provide evidence for regulated minerals used in vehicle electronics, batteries and components.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Materials, coatings, electronics, batteries, plastics, rubber, interior/exterior trim, service parts, supplier BOM/IMDS records and purchasing controls. Smelter/refiner data, supplier declarations and compliance reporting systems.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Restricted substance exceedance, missing supplier declaration, blocked import or sale, recall/field correction, incomplete IMDS/BOM data, customer or regulator finding. Missing smelter data, supplier nonresponse, inaccurate conflict minerals report, customer audit finding, blocked sourcing approval.

## Related Regulations

EU Battery Regulation due diligence; CSRD/CSDDD; OEM supplier codes.
