## Article 12 — Running Gear

① Pneumatic tires of a motor vehicle shall comply with the standards in Attached Table 1-2. [Amended 2014-01-02, 2017-01-09, 2022-10-26]

② All components of the tires and other running gear of a motor vehicle shall be securely joined and shall be free from damage such as cracking, splitting, or excessive corrosion.

③ Motor vehicles (excluding passenger cars) shall have mudguards attached to the rear of the wheels. [Amended 1995-07-21]

④ Wheels fitted to passenger cars and buses, trucks, and special-purpose vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 3.5 tonnes or less (limited to towed vehicles) shall comply with the standards under Article 112-11, and shall be constructed so that brake lining wear can be inspected without removing the wheel. However, ultra-compact vehicles are excluded. [Added 2017-01-09, 2018-07-11]
