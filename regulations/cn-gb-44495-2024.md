# Technical Requirements for Vehicle Cybersecurity

**Regulated Area:** Cybersecurity and software updates

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Manage cyber risk and ensure software updates are authorized, traceable, safe, and recoverable.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

E/E architecture, gateways, ECUs, telematics, OTA backend, diagnostic interfaces, bootloaders, keys/certificates, update campaign tools.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Unauthorized control, data compromise, OTA bricking, wrong software version, update abort, insecure diagnostic access, fleet-wide defect propagation.

## Related Regulations

UNECE R155/R156; GB 44495/44496; KMVSS Arts. 18-4/18-5; VCA/EU/UK type-approval implementation where applicable.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory cybersecurity requirements published 2024 and implemented 2026-01-01 per official listing.
