# Power-Driven Vehicles Manufacturer's Plate

**Regulated Area:** Certification labeling / vehicle identification

**Applicability:** Applies to power-driven vehicles in China; passenger-vehicle relevance is manufacturer plate content, placement and traceability. This is a recommended GB/T standard unless referenced by mandatory approval rules.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide standardized manufacturer identification information on vehicles.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Manufacturer's plate, VIN link, vehicle type/model information, GVWR/mass data, label material and placement.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Missing or inaccurate manufacturer's plate; mismatch with VIN/approval documents; label illegible or not durable; registration or inspection issue.

## Related Regulations

49 CFR Part 567; ADR/RAV compliance data; CMVSS labels; EU manufacturer/statutory plate requirements.

## Engineering Considerations

Mark as GB/T; confirm whether mandatory through CCC/type-approval implementation rules for the specific passenger vehicle program.
