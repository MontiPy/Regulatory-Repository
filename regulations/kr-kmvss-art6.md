## Article 6 — Gross Vehicle Weight, Axle Load, and Wheel Load

① The gross vehicle weight shall not exceed 20 tonnes (30 tonnes for buses, and 40 tonnes for trucks and special-purpose vehicles), the axle load shall not exceed 10 tonnes, and the wheel load shall not exceed 5 tonnes. [Amended 2004-08-06, 2021-08-27]

② The limits for gross vehicle weight, axle load, and wheel load specified in paragraph ① shall also apply to articulated vehicles. [Amended 2021-08-27]

③ The vehicle curb weight for ultra-compact passenger cars shall not exceed 600 kilograms, for ultra-compact trucks 750 kilograms, and for ultra-compact special-purpose vehicles 1,100 kilograms. [Amended 2024-11-29]
