# Road Illumination Devices and Systems for Motor Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Lighting and signaling

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Enable drivers to see and be seen and communicate maneuvers consistently.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Headlamps, DRLs, stop/tail/turn/reverse lamps, reflectors, controllers, switches, wiring, lamp leveling/aiming, tell-tales.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Lighting nonconformance, low intensity, excessive glare, wrong color, wrong mounting height, inoperative lamp, incorrect tell-tale.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 108; UNECE R48/R148/R149/R150; GB 4785/4599/5920; KMVSS Arts. 38/112-4; ADR 1/6/13; CONTRAN 970; GSO 1503; Japan Article 32.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory headlamp/road illumination device standard; implementation 2025-07-01 per official listing.
