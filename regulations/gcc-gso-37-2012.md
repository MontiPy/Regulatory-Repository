# Motor Vehicles - Methods of Test for Impact Strength - Part 2: Rear Impact

**Regulated Area:** Rear impact / fuel integrity

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent post-crash fire/electrical hazards and preserve rear occupant protection in rear impacts.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Rear body structure, fuel tank, filler neck, lines, EV battery rear protection, rear seats, latches, rear bumper.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fuel leakage after impact, battery enclosure breach, HV isolation loss, rear seat detachment, rear closure deformation affecting evacuation.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 301; GB 20072; CONTRAN 910; GSO 37; Japan Article 15; KMVSS Art. 91.

## Engineering Considerations

Listed in GSO MY2027 technical regulations; verify full text for vehicle-category scope.
