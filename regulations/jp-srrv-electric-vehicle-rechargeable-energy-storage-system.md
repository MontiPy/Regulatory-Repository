# Electric Vehicle Rechargeable Energy Storage System

**Regulated Area:** Fuel / EV / high-voltage safety

**Applicability:** EV battery, HV distribution, charging, BMS, HV labels, post-crash isolation, service disconnects Retained under broad-scope review so the program team can manually disposition applicability.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent fire, explosion, shock and hazardous leakage during normal use, abuse and post-crash conditions.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Fuel system, tanks, lines, evaporative components, HV battery/REESS, contactors, service disconnect, inverters, hydrogen/CNG storage and crash shutoff controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fuel leakage after impact, battery thermal event, loss of high-voltage isolation, electrolyte leakage, contactor weld, pressure relief malfunction, fire risk.

## Related Regulations

Possible functional overlap: Japan MLIT / Safety Regulations Article 17-2 - Electrical Devices / High Voltage Protection

## Engineering Considerations

Regional passenger/light-vehicle coverage; confirm exact applicability by vehicle category, model year, fuel/propulsion type, and local type-approval route.
