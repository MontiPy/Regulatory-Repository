# Automotive quality management system

**Regulated Area:** Quality management / CoP evidence

**Applicability:** Not a government regulation by itself, but commonly required by OEM/customer and supports CoP, APQP, PPAP and production control evidence for passenger-vehicle programs.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide a structured quality system that supports regulatory production conformity and supplier-quality evidence.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Quality management system, APQP/PPAP, control plans, supplier management, plant quality, audit records and corrective-action system.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Uncontrolled process change, CoP drift, supplier quality escape, missing audit evidence, launch quality defect or customer audit finding.

## Related Regulations

ISO 9001; UNECE CoP; EU 2018/858; national CoP audits.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence, not as a legal vehicle standard.
