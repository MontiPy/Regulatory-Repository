# Automated-driving scenario, safety and AI guidance standards

**Regulated Area:** Automated driving / ADAS safety evidence

**Applicability:** Not generally direct government regulations, but relevant evidence for ADS/ADAS scenario-based safety cases where automated-driving features are developed.


## Key Compliance Intent

Support scenario-based safety evidence for ADAS and automated-driving functions.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

E/E architecture, ECUs, telematics, gateways, ADAS/automated-driving controllers, diagnostics, software release records, update backend and cybersecurity controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Scenario coverage gap, unsafe ODD definition, weak fallback strategy, AI model performance issue, approval evidence challenge.

## Related Regulations

UN R157; EU GSR2; ISO 21448; ISO 26262.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as emerging supporting evidence for future AD/ADAS approvals.
