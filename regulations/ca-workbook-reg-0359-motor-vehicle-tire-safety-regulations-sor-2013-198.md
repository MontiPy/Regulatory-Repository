# Motor Vehicle Tire Safety Regulations

**Regulated Area:** Tires and wheels / traceability / tire compliance

**Applicability:** Applies to tires for motor vehicles, including passenger-vehicle tire identification, National Safety Mark, tire information, records, registration and import requirements.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure tires supplied for Canadian passenger vehicles are identifiable, traceable and supported by records for safety compliance and recalls.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Tires, sidewall markings, tire information, tire records, dealer registration systems, import and service tire controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Missing or incorrect tire identification; Canadian tire mark mismatch; customer registration not captured; tire recall population incomplete; imported tire nonconformance.

## Related Regulations

49 CFR Part 574; FMVSS 110/139; UNECE R30; CMVSS tire-related standards.

## Engineering Considerations

Direct Canadian counterpart to the U.S. tire identification/recordkeeping gap.
