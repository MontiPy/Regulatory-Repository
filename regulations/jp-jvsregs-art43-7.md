## Article 43-7 — Vehicle Approach Warning Device

① Motor vehicles equipped with an electrically powered engine (excluding two-wheeled vehicles, sidecar-equipped two-wheeled vehicles, three-wheeled vehicles, light vehicles with crawler tracks and sleds, large special vehicles, small special vehicles, and towed vehicles) shall be equipped with a vehicle approach warning device that complies with the standards for function, performance, etc. prescribed by ministerial notice, so as to notify pedestrians and others of the vehicle's approach. However, this does not apply to vehicles whose internal combustion engine operates at all times while in motion.
