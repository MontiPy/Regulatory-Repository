# Performance and Installation Requirements of Devices for Indirect Vision of Motor Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Mirrors and indirect vision

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide required rearward, side, and indirect fields of view for safe driving and maneuvering.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Exterior mirrors, interior mirror, camera-monitor systems, displays, blind-spot view devices, mounts, heaters, software.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Blind zone, distorted image, camera display delay, display blanking, mirror detachment, insufficient rear/side visibility.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 111; UNECE R46; GB 15084; KMVSS Art. 94; ADR 14; CONTRAN 966; Japan Article 44.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory mirror/camera indirect vision requirements; vehicle display latency and FOV are key for CMS.
