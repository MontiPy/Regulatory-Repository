# Protection of Motor Vehicles Against Unauthorized Use

**Regulated Area:** Theft prevention / security

**Applicability:** Immobilizer, keys, locks, BCM, steering locks, VIN/marking, alarm suppliers Retained under broad-scope review so the program team can manually disposition applicability.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent vehicle theft/unauthorized operation and unintended movement when parked.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Keys/fobs, immobilizer, steering/shift lock, alarm, body control module, gateway, cybersecurity and vehicle authorization logic.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Immobilizer bypass, false lock/unlock, rollaway, alarm nuisance or non-function, unauthorized start, key-pairing failure.

## Engineering Considerations

Regional passenger/light-vehicle coverage; confirm exact applicability by vehicle category, model year, fuel/propulsion type, and local type-approval route.
