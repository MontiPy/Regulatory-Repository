# Motor Vehicles - Windshield Demisting and Defrosting Systems

**Regulated Area:** Defrosting and demisting

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Restore and maintain forward visibility in cold or humid conditions.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

HVAC blower, ducts, windshield outlets, heater core/heat pump, rear defogger grid, sensors, HVAC controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Defrost/demist nonperformance, persistent fogging or ice, insufficient airflow, blocked vents, delayed visibility recovery.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 103; GB 11555; ADR 42; CONTRAN 224; SASO 3005; Japan Article 45.
