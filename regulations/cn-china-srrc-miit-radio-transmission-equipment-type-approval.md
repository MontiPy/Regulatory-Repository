# China Telecom/RF - SRRC radio transmission equipment approval

**Regulated Area:** Telecom / RF / EMC

**Applicability:** Applies to radio transmission equipment placed on the China market; passenger vehicles are relevant where connected, keyless, telematics, radar or other radio-transmitting modules are installed.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure radio devices fitted to passenger vehicles comply with market spectrum, EMC and equipment authorization rules.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Telematics ECU, cellular modem, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi/UWB/NFC modules, keyless entry, TPMS, radar sensors, antennas, wiring harnesses, cybersecurity gateway and labels.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Illegal transmitter configuration, RF interference, failed type approval, missing label or user notice, degraded keyless/telematics performance, customs or sales hold.

## Related Regulations

GB cybersecurity/data rules; FCC Part 15; EU RED; Canada ISED; Japan MIC/TELEC; Korea RRA; Brazil ANATEL.
