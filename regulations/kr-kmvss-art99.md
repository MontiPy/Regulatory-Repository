## Article 99 — Head Restraints

Head restraints on the front seats (excluding center seats) and any other seats equipped with head restraints in vehicles falling under any of the items in Article 26 shall conform to the detailed standards prescribed by the Minister of Land, Infrastructure and Transport. However, head restraints on folding auxiliary seats, side-facing seats, and rear-facing seats are excluded. [Amended 2013-03-23]
