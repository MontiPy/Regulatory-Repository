## Article 111 — Types of Autonomous Driving Systems

Autonomous driving systems are categorized as follows:

1. Partial autonomous driving system: an autonomous driving system that operates the vehicle under specified conditions but requires driver intervention when needed, such as when operational limits are reached.

2. Conditionally fully autonomous driving system: an autonomous driving system that operates the vehicle under specified conditions without driver intervention.

3. Fully autonomous driving system: an autonomous driving system that operates the vehicle in all domains without driver intervention.
