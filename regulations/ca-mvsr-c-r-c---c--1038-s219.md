219(1)Every passenger car, other than a forward control configuration vehicle or an open-body type vehicle with a fold-down or removable windshield, shall conform to the requirements of Technical Standards Document No. 219, Windshield Zone Intrusion (TSD 219), as amended from time to time.(2)Every truck, bus and multi-purpose passenger vehicle with a GVWR of 4 536 kg or less, other than a walk-in van, a forward control configuration vehicle or an open-body type vehicle with a fold-down or removable windshield, shall conform to the requirements of TSD 219, as amended from time to time.(3)[Repealed, SOR/2014-307, s. 22]SOR/81-665, s. 2; SOR/97-201, s. 4; SOR/97-421, ss. 17, 22(F); SOR/2008-72, s. 12(F); SOR/2009-121, s. 4; SOR/2013-9, s. 8; SOR/2014-307, s. 22
