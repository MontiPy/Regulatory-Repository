# Argentina model configuration and environmental configuration licenses

**Regulated Area:** Market access / type approval / emissions

**Applicability:** Applies to new vehicles for public-road circulation in Argentina; passenger vehicle programs require LCM safety and LCA environmental evidence.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger vehicles have required Argentine configuration licenses before sale and registration.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Whole-vehicle approval dossier, type-approval certificates, CoP records, VIN/certification labels, sales/import documentation, owner manuals, dealer documentation. Emissions/environmental license evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Market entry blocked, invalid or incomplete approval file, mismatched variant data, missing local label/manual language, CoP audit finding, delayed launch or customs hold. LCM/LCA rejection or missing environmental license.

## Related Regulations

Mercosur technical regulations; UNECE regulations; Brazil/CONTRAN overlap.
