# Vietnam technical and environmental safety requirements for vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Market access / safety / emissions

**Applicability:** Applies to vehicles manufactured, assembled or imported in Vietnam; passenger vehicle applicability depends on category and effective date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure vehicles entering Vietnam meet local technical safety and environmental requirements.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Whole-vehicle approval dossier, type-approval certificates, CoP records, VIN/certification labels, sales/import documentation, owner manuals, dealer documentation. Emissions and fuel-consumption evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Market entry blocked, invalid or incomplete approval file, mismatched variant data, missing local label/manual language, CoP audit finding, delayed launch or customs hold. Test report or local inspection mismatch.

## Related Regulations

ASEAN APMRA; UNECE regulations; Vietnam QCVN standards.
