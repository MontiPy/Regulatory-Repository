## Article 112-4 — Lighting System Components

Headlamps used in motor vehicles (excluding ultra-compact vehicles) shall meet the luminous intensity standards in Attached Tables 6-3 through 6-5 and the chromaticity standards in Attached Table 6-22. [Amended 2014-06-10, 2018-07-11]
