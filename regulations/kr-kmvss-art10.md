## Article 10 — Ground Contact Area and Ground Pressure

The ground contact area and ground pressure of a motor vehicle in a loaded condition shall comply with the following standards:

1. The ground contact area shall be constructed so as to minimize noise generation and avoid risk of road damage

2. (Deleted) [1999-02-19]

3. The ground pressure of vehicles equipped with continuous tracks shall not exceed 3 kilograms per square centimeter of track contact area
