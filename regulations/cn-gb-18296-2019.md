# Safety Property Requirements and Test Methods for Automobile Fuel Tank and Its Installation

**Regulated Area:** Fuel system integrity

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent fuel release and fire risk during normal use and after crashes.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Fuel tank, filler neck/cap, fuel lines, pump/module, EVAP canister, pressure relief, mounting straps, shielding.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fuel leakage after impact, damaged filler pipe, vapor leak, evaporative emissions failure, post-crash fire hazard.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 301; UNECE R34; GB 18296/20072; KMVSS Arts. 17/91; CONTRAN 910; Japan Article 15.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory fuel tank/installation standard; includes tank integrity and installation safety.
