# Safe Drinking Water and Toxic Enforcement Act warning requirements

**Regulated Area:** Materials / chemical warning / consumer information

**Applicability:** Applies to products that expose persons in California to listed chemicals; passenger vehicle relevance for materials, service parts and customer warnings.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide required consumer warnings and control listed-chemical exposure risk for vehicles, parts and accessories sold in California.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Materials, coatings, electronics, batteries, plastics, rubber, interior/exterior trim, service parts, supplier BOM/IMDS records and purchasing controls. Labels, owner manuals, service literature and retailer/dealer communications.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Restricted substance exceedance, missing supplier declaration, blocked import or sale, recall/field correction, incomplete IMDS/BOM data, customer or regulator finding. Missing or incorrect warning, retailer/customer complaint, enforcement action.

## Related Regulations

REACH; ELV; RoHS; PFAS restrictions; IMDS.

## Engineering Considerations

Added because chemical warning obligations can affect vehicle labels, manuals, service parts and accessories.
