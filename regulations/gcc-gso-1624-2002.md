# Motor Vehicles - Noise Emitted by Motor Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Noise and AVAS

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Limit community noise while ensuring quiet electrified vehicles are detectable by pedestrians.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Engine, exhaust, intake, tires, body sealing, acoustic covers, AVAS speaker/controller, exterior sound calibration.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Excessive exterior noise, missing or too-quiet EV warning sound, wrong AVAS activation, tonal noise nonconformance.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 141; UNECE R51/R138; ADR 83/113; GB 1495; KMVSS Art. 35; GSO 1624; Japan Articles 30/43-7.

## Engineering Considerations

Exterior noise requirements; country-specific enforcement may vary.
