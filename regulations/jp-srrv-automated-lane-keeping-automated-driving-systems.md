# Automated Lane Keeping / Automated Driving Systems

**Regulated Area:** Active safety / braking / ADAS

**Applicability:** Camera/radar/lidar/ultrasonic, ADAS ECUs, HMI warnings, driver monitoring, data logging Retained under broad-scope review so the program team can manually disposition applicability.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure the vehicle and compliance record can be legally placed on market, traced, recalled and reported to authorities.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Compliance documentation, VIN/certification labels, owner manuals, type approval files, recall systems, dealer/importer records, production traceability data.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Incorrect VIN/label, incomplete type-approval evidence, recall population not traceable, missing owner notification, delayed reporting, import/conformity hold.

## Related Regulations

Possible functional overlap: Japan MLIT / Safety Regulations Article 10 - Control Devices

## Engineering Considerations

Regional passenger/light-vehicle coverage; confirm exact applicability by vehicle category, model year, fuel/propulsion type, and local type-approval route.
