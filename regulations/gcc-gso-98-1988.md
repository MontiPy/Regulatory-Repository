# Motor Vehicles - Flammability of Interior Materials and Testing Methods

**Regulated Area:** Flammability

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Slow interior flame spread to improve occupant escape time and reduce fire injury severity.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Seats, carpets, headliner, door trim, insulation, parcel shelf, soft trim, adhesives, NVH pads.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Rapid flame spread, excessive burn rate, flaming debris, nonapproved material substitution, fire growth after ignition.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 302; GSO 98; KMVSS Art. 95; Japan Article 20; material requirements in other regional interior standards.

## Engineering Considerations

Interior trim burn-rate relevance for passenger cabins.
