## Article 90-2 — Electronic Stability Control Performance Standards

The structure, performance, and failure identification marking standards for the electronic stability control (ESC) system of motor vehicles (excluding ultra-compact vehicles) shall meet the standards in Attached Table 7-7. [Amended 2018-07-11]
