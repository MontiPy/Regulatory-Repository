# Motor Vehicles - Impact Strength

**Regulated Area:** Occupant crash protection

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce death and serious injury for belted and unbelted occupants in representative passenger vehicle crashes.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Airbags, seat belts, pretensioners, load limiters, crash sensors, restraint ECU, seats, steering column, interior trim, BIW crash structure.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Excessive occupant injury criteria, airbag non-deployment or late deployment, belt spool-out, submarining, hard interior contact, occupant ejection.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 208, 201; UNECE R94/R137; GB 11551; KMVSS Art. 102; ADR 69/73; CONTRAN 910/964; Japan Article 18.

## Engineering Considerations

General impact strength regulation; use with GSO 36-39 and 1707/1708 test methods.
