# Passenger Automobile and Light Truck Greenhouse Gas Emission Regulations

**Regulated Area:** Emissions / GHG / ZEV / fleet compliance

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger automobiles and light trucks in Canada, with GHG emission standards, fleet average requirements, credits, reports and records.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce GHG emissions from passenger automobiles and light trucks and govern associated fleet compliance obligations.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Engine, hybrid/EV system, battery, road-load data, calibration, fleet compliance ledger, National Emissions Mark, reports and records.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Fleet GHG shortfall; wrong CO2 data; missing ZEV or credit documentation; late report; National Emissions Mark issue; model classification mismatch.

## Related Regulations

40 CFR Parts 86 and 600; 49 CFR Parts 531/533/537; EU CO2/Euro 7.

## Engineering Considerations

Include because Canadian passenger-vehicle compliance is not limited to CMVSS safety standards.
