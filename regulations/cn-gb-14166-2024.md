# Safety-belts and Restraint Systems for Occupants of Power-driven Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Seat belts and anchorages

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Keep occupants restrained and position them correctly for crash energy management and airbag interaction.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Seat belts, buckles, retractors, pretensioners, load limiters, lower anchors, D-rings, floor/B-pillar/body reinforcements.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Anchorage pull-out, belt webbing rupture, buckle unlatch, excessive spool-out, poor belt fit, belt warning faults.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 209/210; UNECE R14/R16; GB 14166/14167; KMVSS Arts. 27/103; ADR 4/5; CONTRAN 951; GSO 96/97; Japan Article 22-3.

## Engineering Considerations
