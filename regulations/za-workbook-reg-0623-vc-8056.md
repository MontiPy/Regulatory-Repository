# Pneumatic tyres for passenger cars and trailers

**Regulated Area:** Tires and wheels / component approval

**Applicability:** Applies to pneumatic tyres for passenger cars and trailers in South Africa; passenger-car tire programs and service tire strategy are directly relevant.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger-car tyres sold or installed for South Africa meet compulsory specification and approval evidence requirements.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Tires, sidewall markings, approval marks, size/load/speed ratings, supplier certificates, tire records and market surveillance evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Incorrect tire mark, invalid approval, wrong load/speed rating, blocked import/sale, tire compliance or recall traceability gap.

## Related Regulations

UNECE R30; FMVSS 139; CMVSS tire rules; ADR tyre standards.

## Engineering Considerations

Specific row added because South African tire specifications can affect vehicle launch and accessory/service tire planning.
