# Automotive software process assessment model

**Regulated Area:** Software quality / process evidence

**Applicability:** Not a government regulation, but commonly used by OEMs/suppliers to demonstrate software development process capability.


## Key Compliance Intent

Support robust software development and supplier assurance for software-intensive vehicle systems.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Software development process, requirements, architecture, verification, change management, release records and supplier engineering controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Untraceable requirement, software quality escape, supplier process weakness, release evidence gap, audit finding.

## Related Regulations

ISO 26262; ISO/SAE 21434; ISO 24089; OEM customer requirements.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence, not as a legal vehicle standard.
