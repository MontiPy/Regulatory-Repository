# Motor vehicle right-to-repair telematics data access

**Regulated Area:** Access to repair / connected vehicle data

**Applicability:** Applies to model year 2022 and later vehicles with telematics systems in Massachusetts context; passenger-vehicle relevance depends on telematics content and sales/service model.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure repairers and vehicle owners can access required diagnostic/repair data while managing cybersecurity and privacy risk.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Telematics system, diagnostic data gateway, mobile app/API, cybersecurity controls, service information portal, dealer/aftermarket access process and owner-consent workflow.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Blocked or non-compliant repair data access, cybersecurity exposure, disabled telematics feature, customer/service disruption, regulatory or litigation risk.

## Related Regulations

EU RMI under Regulation (EU) 2018/858; U.S. service information/OBD requirements; state right-to-repair laws.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as a broad connected-vehicle compliance watch item; enforcement posture can change and must be reverified.
