212(1)Every passenger car, other than a forward control configuration vehicle or an open-body type vehicle with a fold-down or removable windshield, shall conform to the requirements of Technical Standards Document No. 212, Windshield Mounting (TSD 212), as amended from time to time.(2)Every truck, bus and multi-purpose passenger vehicle with a GVWR of 4 536 kg or less, other than a walk-in van, a forward control configuration vehicle or an open-body type vehicle with a fold-down or removable windshield, shall conform to the requirements of TSD 212, as amended from time to time.(3)[Repealed, SOR/2014-307, s. 19]SOR/80-282, s. 3; SOR/82-274, s. 1; SOR/90-387, s. 2(F); SOR/93-5, s. 6; SOR/94-692, s. 2(F); SOR/95-536, s. 7(F); SOR/97-201, s. 5; SOR/97-421, ss. 17, 22(F); SOR/2003-57, s. 2; SOR/2008-72, s. 10(F); SOR/2009-121, s. 3; SOR/2013-9, s. 7; SOR/2014-307, s. 19
