# India passenger-vehicle type approval and AIS safety framework

**Regulated Area:** Market access / type approval

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles manufactured/imported/sold in India; passenger cars require type approval and relevant AIS/IS evidence depending on category, powertrain and feature content.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger vehicles placed on the market have recognized safety/environmental approvals, correct documentation and traceable compliance evidence.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Whole-vehicle approval dossier, type-approval certificates, CoP records, VIN/certification labels, sales/import documentation, owner manuals, dealer documentation.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Market entry blocked, invalid or incomplete approval file, mismatched variant data, missing local label/manual language, CoP audit finding, delayed launch or customs hold.

## Related Regulations

UNECE regulations; FMVSS evidence where accepted by local authority; India AIS/IS standards.
