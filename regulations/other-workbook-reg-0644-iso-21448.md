# Road vehicles - Safety of the intended functionality

**Regulated Area:** SOTIF / ADAS safety evidence

**Applicability:** Not a direct government regulation, but relevant to ADAS/automated functions where performance limitations can cause hazards without a component fault.


## Key Compliance Intent

Demonstrate that intended-function limitations are understood, mitigated and validated for passenger-vehicle ADAS/automated functions.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

E/E architecture, ECUs, telematics, gateways, ADAS/automated-driving controllers, diagnostics, software release records, update backend and cybersecurity controls.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

False negative/false positive detection, unsafe ADAS behavior in edge case, inadequate driver handover, approval evidence gap.

## Related Regulations

UN R157; EU GSR2 ADAS rules; ISO 26262; ISO 34502.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence for ADAS/ADS approval readiness.
