# Motor Vehicle Safety Regulations - Certification, Records, Importation and Defect/Non-compliance Provisions

**Regulated Area:** Certification / market entry / recall

**Applicability:** Applies to prescribed classes of vehicles and regulated equipment, including passenger cars and many passenger-vehicle derivatives sold or imported in Canada.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide the legal framework for applying CMVSS, certification labels, records, imports and corrective action for Canadian passenger vehicles.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Compliance label, National Safety Mark, owner manual content, records, import documents, defect and non-compliance reporting systems.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Missing or wrong compliance label; incomplete records; incorrect imported-vehicle declaration; late defect/non-compliance notice; mismatch between CMVSS evidence and vehicle configuration.

## Related Regulations

CMVSS Schedule III standards; U.S. 49 CFR Parts 565, 567, 573, 577; EU type approval.
