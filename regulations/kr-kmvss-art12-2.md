## Article 12-2 — Tire Pressure Warning System

① Passenger cars and vans, trucks, and special-purpose vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 3.5 tonnes or less shall be equipped with a tire pressure warning system. However, vehicles fitted with dual wheels (複輪), towed vehicles, and ultra-compact vehicles are excluded. [Amended 2018-07-11]

② The tire pressure warning system shall comply with the following standards: [Amended 2020-12-24, 2021-08-27]

1. It shall operate at a minimum in the speed range from 40 km/h up to the maximum speed of the vehicle.

2. The warning lamp shall comply with the following standards:

a. It shall illuminate when the ignition key is in the engine-running position and extinguish when conditions are normal. However, this does not apply to identification symbols displayed in a shared zone.

b. The warning lamp shall be readily visible to the driver by the naked eye from the driver's seat even in daylight.

[Added 2011-03-16]
