# Automotive core quality tools

**Regulated Area:** Quality planning / supplier quality evidence

**Applicability:** Not government regulations, but commonly required by OEMs and suppliers to support APQP, risk management, production readiness and CoP evidence.


## Key Compliance Intent

Provide structured product/process risk control and evidence for production readiness.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

DFMEA, PFMEA, control plans, measurement systems, process capability, PPAP packages, supplier launch readiness and corrective-action records.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Uncontrolled failure mode, measurement error, incapable process, PPAP rejection, launch spill or field quality issue.

## Related Regulations

IATF 16949; CoP frameworks; ISO 9001.

## Engineering Considerations

Added as supporting evidence, not as a legal vehicle standard.
