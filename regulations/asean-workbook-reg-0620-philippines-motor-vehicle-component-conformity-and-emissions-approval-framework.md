# Philippines vehicle and component certification, COC and emissions/environmental framework

**Regulated Area:** Market access / type approval / emissions

**Applicability:** Applies to vehicles/components sold or registered in the Philippines; passenger vehicles require market-specific conformity and emissions evidence.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure passenger vehicles and regulated components meet Philippine conformity and emissions requirements before sale/registration.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Whole-vehicle approval dossier, type-approval certificates, CoP records, VIN/certification labels, sales/import documentation, owner manuals, dealer documentation. Emissions COC and component certification evidence.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Market entry blocked, invalid or incomplete approval file, mismatched variant data, missing local label/manual language, CoP audit finding, delayed launch or customs hold. Missing COC, product certification gap, customs/registration hold.

## Related Regulations

ASEAN APMRA; UNECE regulations; national PNS/BPS and EMB requirements.
