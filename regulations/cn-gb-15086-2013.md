# Motor Vehicles - Door Locks and Door Retention Components

**Regulated Area:** Door locks, hinges, and retention

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Keep closures secured in crashes while enabling safe access or egress when required.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Side door latches, hinges, strikers, handles, hood latch, trunk latch, liftgate, power latch controls, child locks.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Door opening during crash, hood fly-up, trunk entrapment, latch stuck closed, occupant ejection risk, water/dust-induced latch corrosion.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 206/113/401; UNECE R11; GB 15086; KMVSS Arts. 21/104; ADR 2/29; CONTRAN 885/908; Japan body and closure articles.

## Engineering Considerations

Current mandatory door lock/retention standard with revision tracking needed.
