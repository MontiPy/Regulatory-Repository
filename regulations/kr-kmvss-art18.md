## Article 18 — Electrical Equipment

The electrical equipment of a motor vehicle shall comply with the following standards:

1. All electrical wiring of the vehicle shall be covered with insulating material and fixed to the vehicle body.

2. Electrical terminals and electrical switches inside the passenger compartment shall be appropriately covered with insulating material.

3. The battery shall be fixed so that it is not loosened or damaged by vehicle vibration or shock, and batteries installed inside the passenger compartment shall be covered with insulating material.
