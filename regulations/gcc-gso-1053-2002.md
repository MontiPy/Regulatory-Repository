# Motor Vehicles - Protection Against Unauthorized Use

**Regulated Area:** Theft prevention / immobilizer

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce theft and unintended vehicle movement by requiring effective locking and immobilization features.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Immobilizer, key/fob, steering lock, start authorization, alarm, body control module, door locks, diagnostic access.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Vehicle can be started without valid key, steering lock bypass, immobilizer false no-start, alarm nuisance, rollaway due control logic.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 114; UNECE R116/R161/R162/R163; ADR 25/82; GSO 1053; KMVSS Art. 22; Japan Article 11-2; CONTRAN 245/330 needs current check.
