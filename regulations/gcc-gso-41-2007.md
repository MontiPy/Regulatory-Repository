# Front and Rear Exterior Protection Devices for Passenger Cars and Methods of Test

**Regulated Area:** Bumpers and exterior projections

**Applicability:** Applies to motor vehicles exported/registered in GCC member states when listed in GSO motor vehicle technical regulations; passenger relevance noted for passenger cars/M1.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce minor-impact damage and avoid exterior features that increase injury risk.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Bumper beams, fascias, crash boxes, exterior trim, grilles, spoilers, tow hooks, pedestrian contact surfaces.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Sharp edge exposure, excessive low-speed damage, bumper misalignment, pedestrian contact hard points, sensor/ADAS misalignment after bump.

## Related Regulations

CMVSS 215; ADR 92; GSO 41; Japan Article 18; UNECE R26/R42 where adopted; FMVSS bumper standard is 49 CFR Part 581, not FMVSS Part 571.

## Engineering Considerations

Explicit passenger car relevance for bumpers/exterior protection devices.
