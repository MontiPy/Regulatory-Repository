# The Protection of Motor Vehicle for Pedestrians in the Event of a Collision

**Regulated Area:** Pedestrian protection

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce pedestrian head and leg injury in low-speed frontal impacts involving passenger vehicles.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Hood, fenders, bumper fascia/beam, hood hinges/latches, active hood, windshield base/cowl, AEB sensors where required.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

High head injury criterion, high tibia/knee loading, stiff bumper response, active hood non-deployment, exposed hard points.

## Related Regulations

UNECE R127; GB 24550; KMVSS Art. 102-2; Japan Article 18; ADR 92/98/107 where applicable.

## Engineering Considerations

Mandatory pedestrian protection; current implementation status should be confirmed for launch timing.
