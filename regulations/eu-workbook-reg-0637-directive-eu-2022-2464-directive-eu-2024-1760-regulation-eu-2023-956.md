# CSRD, CSDDD and CBAM sustainability/due-diligence overlays

**Regulated Area:** Sustainability / supply-chain reporting

**Applicability:** Potentially applies at corporate, supply-chain or importer level rather than vehicle type approval; passenger-vehicle programs may be affected through materials, supplier and carbon data requirements.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure vehicle programs generate and retain supplier/material/carbon data needed for corporate and market-access sustainability obligations.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Supplier management, materials/BOM data, carbon accounting, procurement, logistics, battery/material sourcing and corporate reporting systems.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Missing carbon/materials data, supplier nonconformance, reporting gap, import cost exposure, customer audit issue.

## Related Regulations

EU Battery Regulation; ELV; REACH; conflict minerals; national due-diligence laws.

## Engineering Considerations

Intentionally broad. Treat as a program data/evidence requirement, not a CBU validation requirement.
