# MLIT Vehicle Recall and Improvement Measures

**Regulated Area:** Recall / field action / quality

**Applicability:** Applies to vehicles in Japan, including passenger vehicles, when defects or nonconformities require recall, improvement measures or service campaigns.


## Key Compliance Intent

Ensure safety-related defects are reported and corrected in the Japanese market.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Field quality system, incident reports, recall population, owner notification, dealer remedy instructions, regulator reporting.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Late recall; wrong affected population; incomplete owner notification; remedy not validated; recurring safety issue in field.

## Related Regulations

49 CFR Parts 573/577; EU market surveillance; Canada/Australia recall rules; Korea/Brazil recall frameworks.

## Engineering Considerations

Use Japan-specific terminology and campaign classifications during implementation.
