# Performance Requirements and Test Methods of Electronic Stability Control System for Light Vehicles

**Regulated Area:** Electronic stability control / ADAS

**Applicability:** Applies to passenger vehicles when the GB standard scope includes M1/passenger cars or road motor vehicles; verify mandatory vs recommended status and implementation date.


## Key Compliance Intent

Reduce loss-of-control and avoid or mitigate collisions through controlled intervention.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

ESC ECU, yaw/acceleration sensors, steering angle sensor, brake modulator, powertrain torque control, AEBS/ELKS cameras/radar.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Spinout or plow-out, delayed ESC intervention, nuisance braking, no AEB on target, lane departure without warning, sensor calibration faults.

## Related Regulations

FMVSS/CMVSS 126; UNECE R140/R152; GB/T 30677; KMVSS Arts. 15-2/90-2/15-3; ADR 88/98/107; CONTRAN 954; Japan Article 12.

## Engineering Considerations

Recommended ESC standard; used for engineering validation even if regulatory implementation cross-references vary.
