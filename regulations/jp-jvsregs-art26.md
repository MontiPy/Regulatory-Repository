## Article 26 — Emergency Exits

① Vehicles exclusively for transporting young children and vehicles with a seating capacity of 30 or more passengers (excluding emergency vehicles) shall be provided with emergency exits that comply with the standards for installation location, size, etc. prescribed by ministerial notice, so as to allow easy evacuation in an emergency. However, this does not apply to vehicles where all seats can be accessed directly from the boarding entrance.

② Motor vehicles equipped with emergency exits shall display the location of the emergency exit and the method of opening the door in a clearly visible manner at or near the emergency exit. In this case, where the location of the emergency exit is indicated by lighting, the color of the light shall be green.

③ Motor vehicles equipped with emergency exits shall be fitted with a device that warns the driver when the emergency exit door is opened.
