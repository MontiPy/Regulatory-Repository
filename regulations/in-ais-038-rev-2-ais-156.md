# India electric powertrain / traction battery safety standards

**Regulated Area:** EV battery and high-voltage safety

**Applicability:** Relevant to electrified vehicle programs in India; AIS-038 Rev.2 is generally associated with M/N category electric powertrain safety, while AIS-156 should be applicability-filtered because it can be category-specific.


## Key Compliance Intent

Prevent EV battery fire, electric shock and unsafe electric-powertrain behavior in Indian-market passenger vehicle programs.

## Primary Vehicle Systems and Components

Traction battery, BMS, battery enclosure, HV wiring, contactors, charging inlet, thermal management, abuse protection and warning labels.

## Failure Modes and Symptoms

Battery thermal event, venting/fire, loss of HV isolation, failed charge protection, inadequate enclosure retention, missing warning/label, approval blocked.

## Related Regulations

UN R100; GB 38031; FMVSS 305; ISO 6469; UN 38.3.

## Engineering Considerations

Kept broad to avoid missing EV program obligations; filter AIS-156 carefully for passenger-car applicability.
