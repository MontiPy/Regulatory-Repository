## Article 87 — Accelerator Control

The accelerator control system of passenger cars and vans, trucks, and special-purpose vehicles with a gross vehicle weight of 4.5 tonnes or less shall comply with the following standards after stabilisation for 12 hours at ambient temperatures between -18°C and 52°C: [Amended 2021-08-27]

1. When the operating force is removed from the accelerator pedal, or when one return power source is disconnected and the operating force is removed: the system shall return from the accelerated position to the idle state within 1 second from the time the driver removes the operating force.

2. For an electrically controlled accelerator control system, when any part of the control circuit is broken or short-circuited: the system shall return from the accelerated position to the idle state within 1 second from the time the driver removes the operating force, or from the time the break or short circuit occurs.

[Fully amended 2014-06-10]
